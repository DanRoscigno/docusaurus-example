Babies and Kids#

Insurance#

forsikringer.no - Collect your insurance - save thousands! Now you can compare terms and find the best deals quickly and easily.

Gravidforsikring - Get support and security both during and after pregnancy. The insurance applies from week 16 of pregnancy, and until the child turns 6 months.

Law#

Advokat.no - Can give you free legal aid. Specialists in labor law, family law, property law, tort cases and much more.

Jushjelpa.no - Free legal aid is an aid for people in difficult financial situations. The solution is for the state to cover all or part of the cost you have in connection with legal assistance.

Norwegian Language#

Klar Tale - Klar Tale is a Norwegian easy-to-read newspaper. The newspaper tries to make the news understandable to everyone. The language is simple, and the letters are larger than in other newspapers.

Norwegian on the Web NoW by NTNU - Norwegian on the Web NoW, Learn Norwegian, online tutorial with audio, listen and repeat, pronunciation rules, vocabulary, grammar, exercises, and extra material

Norwegian as Foreign Language - links - A bunch of useful links to websites where you can learn norwegian.

Supernytt - Is a news program for children who go on NRK Super target group. The program aims to explain what is happening in Norway and the world so that children understand and are able to put things in contex

Personal Finance#

Bytt.no - Compare thousands of prices, services and user reviews and switch to a better and cheaper company.

Referansebudsjettet - SIFO's reference budget shows general consumption expenditure for different types of households. Calculate your own budget!

Smartepenger.no - Smart Money is first and foremost about your finances. Learn how to increase your income or save more money. The possibilities are many, and you can start today. A positive attitude towards money makes life better.

Realestate#

boligsvindel.no - STOP housing fraud Norway, page for the unfortunate home buyer.

STOPP boligsvindel (Facebook Group) - DO YOU NEED HELP AFTER PURCHASING HOMES? Or maybe you are looking for a home and want good advice? Are you having a hard time after buying a home because you were unlucky or cheated, and are you fighting against the home seller insurance companies?
